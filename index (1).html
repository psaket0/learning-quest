Accessible Learning for All

Donated Over 0 Books to 1000+ children in India.

Donate Money

Even the smallest of donations can make the biggest impact for an underprivileged child's learning success! We use the money to buy textbooks, education supplies or for shipping costs.


Donate Books

Any gently used books, textbooks can be a great way to contribute to this cause. Any genres are welcome with recommended age group between 5-15 years age.

Donate Books

Register as a Receiver

Schools or any other institution of learning are eligible to register as a receiver so they may directly coordinate with the volunteer.

Show Your Contributions

Donors can record their books donated through our google form and will be able to take part in our book donation hall of fame as well as our overall donations made.

Start a Chapter

Want to donate outside of Pune area, India and know any other needy areas? With "Start a Chapter", you can coordinate with learning institutions in that area and register as a chapter lead. Email us to know more.

About Us

Learning Quest is a non-profit initiative focusing on distributing books donated by people, schools and libraries from the USA to underprivileged children in India.
